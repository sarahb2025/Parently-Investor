02 / 09The Product

The world's first closed-loop parent data ecosystem.

Every event coordinated, every school group joined, every gift contributed feeds a living dataset that gets richer with every family that joins. Parently is not just a family app. It is the infrastructure for the most valuable consent-led behavioural dataset in the parent economy.

Manual event ticketing tools
Generic group gifting platforms
Facebook Marketplace for school items

Family Directory & Social Network

See who your children know. Photo profiles, family connections, school groups and activity networks in one place.

Real Behavioural Signal

What parents plan, spend, coordinate and delegate in context: intent data at the moment it matters most.

Community Social Graph

Every school, kinder, sports club and activity group builds a rich trust network that enriches every connected family.

The Closed Loop

Parents act inside Parently. Actions generate data. Data improves intelligence. Better intelligence brings more parents. The loop compounds.

Earned, Not Scraped

Collected with explicit consent inside a trusted community. ISO 27001, GDPR and SOC 2 Type 1 compliant from day one.

The AI Advantage

A proprietary parent behavioural dataset is one of the most defensible assets any AI company can build. Parently is building it.

In a world where clean, contextual, consent-led data is scarce, a parent ecosystem rich with behavioural signal across schedules, spending, social graphs and life stages will be invaluable.

Any aggregator serving the family economy: retail, education, health, financial services, will want access to this signal. That is the Parently data business.
